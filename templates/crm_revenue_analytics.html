Data Notes — Phase 8.1
Revenue amounts: Not stored in database. No payment_amount or fee_paid column exists. Catalogue prices in constants.py are display strings only — not per-lead transaction records.
Enquiries: Unique leads with a COURSE_VIEWED, COURSE_ENQUIRY, or COURSE_ADMISSION event for the course.
Admissions: Unique leads with a COURSE_ADMISSION event for the course. Top course KPI excludes courses with zero admissions.
Staff: Uses ConversationState.assigned_staff. Leads with no assignment appear as Unassigned and are excluded from the Top Performing Staff KPI.
Queries: 2 bulk queries total — zero N+1 — all aggregation in Python memory. Read-only, zero writes.
